Deinoccocus Radiodurans

Deinoccocus Radiodurans is an extremophillic bacteria, meaning that it survives in environments most organisms would find to be incredibly harsh. Even by extremophillic standards, Deinoccocus radiodurans is incredibly durable. The bacteria has been observed surviving extreme cold, near complete dehydration, radiation strong enough to kill a human several times over and even the vacuum of space.
